    Our Story

    Who are Remarkable Fungi?


    It started in early 2021 when Darren James Lynch (DJ) first began experimenting with mushroom cultivation in the spare bedroom of his house. As a head chef in a busy Queenstown restaurant and from his love of forging, he noticed that all the mushrooms he was ordering came from different parts of the country and by the time they arrived to the restaurant they were not the best quality due to travel.


    After some research, he soon discovered there were no growers in the Otago basin. That's when the light bulb moment hit and Remarkable Fungi was created.

    It didn't take long for the new business to outgrow the spare bedroom, So with the help of his wife Maggie they moved the production to a double garage in Gibbston Valley. In 2022 the couple quit their full time jobs and decided to take the plunge and move production to a 180m2 warehouse. With this new space the team can focus on producing high quality gourmet mushrooms for retail and wholesale.

    Everyday is a new learning curve here at Remarkable Fungi and we cant wait for you to taste our product.


    DJ
